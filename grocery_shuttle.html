About The Grocery Shuttle

  • Free to all UC San Diego students (undergraduate & graduate)
  • Operates on weekends, every 30 minutes from 10 am to 6 pm during fall, winter and spring quarters
    • Does not operate on university holidays
    • Sixty-minute frequency during the summer

The Origin of the Grocery Shuttle

  • The Grocery Shuttle service was a result of two students' advocacy, Josh & Apollo, through their experience of being houseless students while at UCSD!
    • Josh created the affordable grocery map on an excel sheet that showcased the cost of local culturally reflective grocery stores from UCSD based on afforability during Summer 2018!
    • They yelped reviewed the locations, identified time frames to get to the grocery store, and more!
  • This program is a partnership between Basic Needs, Associated Students (A.S.) and Transportation to improve accessibility of food security resources in the San Diego area!
  • The grocery shuttle takes students to local grocery stores & shopping centers to serve as a resource to access culturally reflective nourishing food!
