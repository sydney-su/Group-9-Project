Trolley Lines

The four different lines of the Trolley include the UC San Diego Blue Line Station, Orange line, Green line, and Copper line.

UC San Diego Blue Line

The UC San Diego Blue line station runs from UTC down to San Ysidro making stops throughout San Diego like Little Italy, San Diego Airport, and downtown.

Orange Line

The Orange line travels from Courthouse station to El Cajon transit center.

Green Line

The Green line travels from Imperial transit center to El Cajon transit center making many stops like Gaslamp, Fashion Valley, Old Town, and through SDSU.

Copper Line

The Copper Line runs from El Cajon to Santee Town Center with only a few stops in between.

More Information

- Popular spots for UC San Diego students to travel are using the Blue line and travelling to UTC mall and Nobel Drive.

- The Trolley stops directly on UCSD campus and makes it easy and accessible for students to travel to surrounding shopping areas for groceries, fun, and a variety of cuisine.

- Another popular stop that is available using the Trolley is Little Italy. There are many restaurants and a farmers market that can be easily accessed through the Trolley stop in Little Italy.

- UCSD students can travel using the Trolley for free through the Pronto app.
